About us

Eventify is a premier event planning company with years of experience in creating memorable events. From weddings to corporate events, we handle it all

Our mission

At Eventify, our mission is to turn your dreams into unforgettable experiences. We are passionate about crafting events that leave a lasting impression, whether it’s an elegant wedding, a dynamic corporate event, or a fun-filled party. Our team is dedicated to bringing your vision to life with creativity, precision, and care.

We believe that every event should be as unique as the people celebrating it. That’s why we focus on personalized service, ensuring that every detail reflects your style and exceeds your expectations. With Eventify, your event is in expert hands, from concept to completion.

Our Story

Eventify began with a simple idea: to make every occasion extraordinary. Founded by a team of passionate event planners, we started with small, intimate gatherings and quickly grew into a full-service event planning company. Our journey has been fueled by a love for creativity and a commitment to excellence.

Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of planning countless weddings, corporate events, and parties, each one as special as the last. Our story is about the people we’ve met, the events we’ve crafted, and the memories we’ve helped create. With every event, we continue to build a legacy of joy and celebration.

FAQs

Take a look at what our customers usually ask us when they first get in touch with us

What services are included in the wedding planning package?

Our wedding planning package includes venue selection, vendor coordination, décor, catering, entertainment, timeline creation, and day-of coordination to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Can I book corporate event planning with specific business needs in mind?

Yes, we offer customized solutions for corporate events tailored to the unique goals and objectives of your business. Whether it’s a conference, seminar, or team-building event, we work to ensure that your event aligns with your corporate objectives.

How early should I start planning a children’s party?

We recommend starting the planning process for a children’s party at least 2-3 weeks in advance to allow time for developing a unique theme, booking entertainers, and organizing all the activities. This also provides sufficient time for all necessary preparations.

Can you assist with organizing parties for small groups or private events?

Absolutely! We organize parties of all sizes, from small private gatherings to large celebrations. Regardless of the scale of the event, we ensure that every detail is considered and your party will be executed to the highest standard.
