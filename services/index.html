FAQs

Take a look at what our customers usually ask us when they first get in touch with us

What services are included in the wedding planning package?

Our wedding planning package includes venue selection, vendor coordination, décor, catering, entertainment, timeline creation, and day-of coordination to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Can I book corporate event planning with specific business needs in mind?

Yes, we offer customized solutions for corporate events tailored to the unique goals and objectives of your business. Whether it’s a conference, seminar, or team-building event, we work to ensure that your event aligns with your corporate objectives.

How early should I start planning a children’s party?

We recommend starting the planning process for a children’s party at least 2-3 weeks in advance to allow time for developing a unique theme, booking entertainers, and organizing all the activities. This also provides sufficient time for all necessary preparations.

Can you assist with organizing parties for small groups or private events?

Absolutely! We organize parties of all sizes, from small private gatherings to large celebrations. Regardless of the scale of the event, we ensure that every detail is considered and your party will be executed to the highest standard.
